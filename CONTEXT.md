# Context

## Mission

**sayakatolik.com** is an Indonesian-language Roman Catholic blog. Its purpose is
**evangelization and catechesis** — to share the Catholic faith clearly, accurately,
and accessibly with every visitor and seeker, in natural Bahasa Indonesia.

## Editorial & doctrinal standard

All content must be faithful to the teaching of the Roman Catholic Church.

- **Doctrine** is checked against the *Catechism of the Catholic Church* (KGK /
  CCC).
- **Scripture** uses the Catholic canon in the Indonesian *LAI Terjemahan Baru*
  (TB), including the deuterocanonical books. Verify every quotation and reference
  against the TB. Reserve quotation marks for verbatim text; present paraphrases as
  glosses, not as quotes.
- **Tradition vs. Scripture:** non-Scriptural devotional details (e.g. the
  Stations of the Cross) are labeled *"Tradisi"* rather than presented as Scripture.
- **Diction:** use correct Catholic Indonesian terminology and official prayer
  translations.

## Glossary

- **Peristiwa** — the preferred term for the mysteries of the Rosary (Peristiwa
  Gembira, Terang, Sedih, Mulia), in place of "Misteri."
- **Meditasi Naratif** — the narrative meditation variant of a Peristiwa.
- **Meditasi Kitab Suci** — the Scripture-quote meditation variant of a Peristiwa.
